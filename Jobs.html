How will Virtual Reality affect Jobs

Virtual reality will help jobs like health care, education, and automotive design. Virtual reality will also help train people for high risk jobs like doctors, nurses, or surgeons to make it easier for them to learn hands on. This will allow a less detrimental result if a mistake was made in this circumstance.
